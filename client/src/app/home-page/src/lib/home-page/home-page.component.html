How do you play the train game?

The project that we as team Goofy decided we wanted to do is called: “the train game”. This is a game mostly known among students who have student OV (free traveling). How to play the train game? First you go to a random station you decide on. So let's say you start at Amsterdam Central station. You throw a dice which lands on the number four. You go to platform four and get on the next train that departs from there. You throw the dice again and now it lands on number one. This means you get off the train at the first stop. You are now at Amsterdam Sloterdijk and start over by throwing the dice, go to that platform, etc. In the end you end up somewhere random and can explore the city’s you stopped at.
